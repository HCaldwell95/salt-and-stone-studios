An unparralelled recording experience

Our studio space is meticulously designed to provide the perfect setting for artists and musicians to bring their visions to life.


Studio A

Studio A features a generous 2000 square ft. open-plan recording studio. Equipped with MCI's renowned 500C console and an array of premium outboard gear, including the finest from Neve, API and Universal Audio. You can find multiple isolation rooms and a vocal booth, providing great flexibility. The final feature of Studio A is the MCI JH24 2-inch tape machine, allowing you to re-create the warmth reminiscent of your favourite recordings.


Studio B

Studio B is our mix and edit suite. Outfitted with the latest technology, you can be certain that we can meet your needs. Provided with Studio B is our own "Blake The Sound Guy", who will guide you through this process and share his wealth of knowledge from being in the business for 45+ years.
