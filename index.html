About Your
Recording Studio

Betws-y-Coed, Wales - Est.2021

Salt And Stone Studios is a 2000 square ft. open-plan recording studio. Nestled within the heart of Snowdonia National Park, our century-old-church-turned-recording facility is a testament to the enduring power of music and community.


In our short time, we have been home to many iconic Welsh recording artists including: Stereophonics, Bullet For My Valentine, Catfish And The Bottlemen & many more.


Our studio is comprised of an enchanting live recording space, equipped with an extensive array of both modern and vintage musical instruments and recording gear. Paired with the awe-inspiring vistas of the Snowdonia landscape and the enduring craftmanship of our cherished church studio, the environment fosters an uninterrupted flow of creativity.


We are dedicated to maintaining a stress-free, encouraging atmosphere that is conducive to the creative process. Our approach to mixing is characterised by a positivity that ensures your artistry is celebrated and respected. You can have peace of mind, knowing that your work is entrusted to kindred spirits who share your deep passion and reverence for the arts. Your creative journey is our priority.


The team here at Salt And Stone Studios are proud to introduce our state-of-the-art facilities, able to cater to a wide range of audio experiences, including Stereo 5.1 and 7.1 Surround, and the immersive 7.1.4 format. Our studio is equipped with over a dozen guitar and assorted pedals, 2+ drum kits, a selection of 14 amplifiers and much more. This expansive collection allows us to capture the perfect sounds for your project, turning the recording process into a truly enjoyable and exciting creative endeavour.
